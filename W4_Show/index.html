The Vampire Diaries follows Elena Gilbert, a teenager in the town of Mystic Falls who becomes entangled in a supernatural world after meeting two vampire brothers, Stefan and Damon Salvatore. As she uncovers the town's dark history, she is pulled into a dangerous mix of romance, mystery, and ancient curses.
